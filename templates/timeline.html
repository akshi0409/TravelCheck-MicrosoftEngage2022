Timeline of Project Development

7 May 2022 - 14 May 2022 (1st Sprint)

  • Learnt more about the topics and decided that face recognition was the one which I could relate to.
  • Reserached and read about the various face recognition techniques and algorithms available and implemented it using OpenCV Library, FaceRecognition.js and Tensorflow. Finally decided that I would use OpenCV Library.
  • Started with the ideation of my project. Travel Security is an important issue so decided to work on this, to automate the process of identity check.
  • Decided the functionalities and features that would be needed.
  • Spoke to the mentor to get a clear idea of the topic and the points to keep in mind.

14 May 2022 - 21 May 2022 (2nd Sprint)

  • Researched on the different tech stacks and frameworks that could be used. Initially began with Django framework but after I learnt about Flask, I was more comfortable with the flask framework so I chose that.
  • Started with building the basic functionalities of the app, the forms page and the home page.
  • Implemented python script separately for face recognition. Explored the features provided by OpenCV and implemented them.
  • Started designing the UI of the website. It was a little difficult to decide something that goes with the idea and also the placement of the components and the color combinations.
  • Attended AMA Sessions and workshops by Microsoft Engage which were very helpful.

21 May 2022 to 29 May 2022 (3rd Sprint)

  • Started integrating the frontend and backend part. Didn't have much knowledge about the Flask framework so I learnt how to integrate the python scripts for face recognition and work with them. It was a little difficult at the beginning to display the video feed in the website but eventually I learnt how to do it.
  • Learnt about working with databases and the which one would be the best for this application, decided I would use SQLite. Read articles and saw youtube videos and understood how to store and retrieve data.
  • Finalized and implemented the design, made all the other pages and integrated them.
  • Created the readme file for github, PPT and the Video demo of my application.
